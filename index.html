Introduction
This Experimental Project focused on exploring the new possibility of typography design in the future Augumented Reality (AR) City. Due to the complex and mass-ive information presentation in the AR environment, a new typography hierachy needed to be established for supplying a high quality reading experience in a dynamic media environment in our city. Vision Type Family, a responsive typeface which can follow reader's sight is designed to face this new challenge from the uncertain future.This type experiment doesn't aim at providing a highly executable solution. It explores a possible future about the typography design in a specific environment, and also it is a reaction to our changing media environment.
